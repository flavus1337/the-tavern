# Chapter 1: Descent into the Hold — DM Guide

## Overview

This chapter establishes the threat and tone of the campaign. The party moves from the surface into the first level of Vaelthar's Hold, encountering increasingly organised undead and the first named cultist villain. Keep encounters crisp — the dungeon's atmosphere does the heavy lifting.

---

## Scene 1-1: The Ash Gate

**Read-aloud text:**

> The track ends at a bluff of black granite. Someone has levered open the old iron portcullis — it groans against its chain stops, held at half height. Beyond it the air turns cold and heavy with the smell of char. In the pale ash that dusts the threshold you can count at least a dozen sets of booted prints, all leading inward, none returning.

**DM notes:**

- This scene is a free narrative beat; no combat. Use it to set pacing.
- A DC 12 Wisdom (Perception) check notices the violet-glowing threshold rune just inside the entrance hall doorway. A DC 14 Intelligence (Arcana) check identifies it as a *sentinel trigger* — crossing it will animate any bound undead within 30 feet.
- The portcullis winch is jammed (DC 14 Strength check to lower it, creating a blocking obstacle if the party wants to bottleneck pursuers later).

---

## Scene 1-2: Entrance Hall

**Read-aloud text:**

> Torch brackets hold blackened stubs of tallow. In the two alcoves flanking the far archway, armoured skeletons stand rigid as statues — yellowed bone wrapped in rusted chainmail, spears held at rest. The floor between you and them is inscribed with a rune that pulses with faint violet light.

**DM notes:**

- If the party warned themselves about the trigger rune (Scene 1-1), they can attempt to bypass it: *Dispel Magic* (DC 13) suppresses it for 1 minute; carefully stepping over it (DC 10 Dexterity/Acrobatics) works if they go slowly (one at a time; failure = rune triggers).
- If the rune triggers, both **Skeleton Sentinels** attack immediately (initiative normally). They pursue only to the entrance hall boundary — they are bound to the room.
- Treasure: beneath the right-alcove skeleton's foot is a loose flagstone (DC 14 Perception) covering a small hollow with 12 sp, a black wax seal ring (Malgrath's crest — keep this; it opens the Crypt door in Chapter 2), and a tattered scroll of *Speak with Dead*.

---

## Scene 1-3: The Guard Room

**Read-aloud text:**

> Tables have been shoved against the walls to make space for a rough command post. A large woman with close-cropped white hair and an ash-grey robe stands with her back to you, tracing something on a crumbling parchment pinned to stone. Two skeleton soldiers stand at flanking positions. Without turning: *"Did you recover the eastern shard seal, or am I going to be disappointed again?"*

**DM notes:**

- **Vorra** (Cultist Lieutenant) realises her mistake on the first hostile action; she does not surrender but may attempt to flee toward the Ritual Chamber if reduced below 20 HP. She shouts *"Intruders in the entrance! Sound the excavation halt!"* — this is not mechanically meaningful in Chapter 1 but seeds tension for Chapter 2 (the cult will have heard).
- On her body: coded orders bearing the Ashen Throne symbol (players can decipher with DC 15 Intelligence/History — they learn one additional shard is already in the Ritual Chamber), iron key to the Armoury door, 45 gp, and a *potion of healing*.
- The wall map is the same dungeon map the party may be viewing. Point out the Ritual Chamber label — this is a deliberate hook.
- Roleplaying hook: if Vorra is captured alive and questioned (she will not cooperate easily — DC 14 Charisma/Intimidation or DC 12 Persuasion if the party reveals they oppose Malgrath for personal reasons), she can reveal that a senior cultist named **Serevath** is in the Ritual Chamber conducting the binding ritual. This skips the investigation beat in Chapter 2 Scene 2-1.

## Chapter Pacing

| Scene | Estimated duration | Combat? |
|---|---|---|
| 1-1 The Ash Gate | 10–15 min | No |
| 1-2 Entrance Hall | 20–30 min | Optional |
| 1-3 The Guard Room | 30–45 min | Yes (likely) |

End the session after Scene 1-3 to land on the cliffhanger of the coded orders and the map. Begin Chapter 2 with the party choosing their next room.
